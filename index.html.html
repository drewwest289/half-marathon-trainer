How to export from Health Auto Export
Open Health Auto Export → tap Export → choose a date range → select JSON format.
Open the exported file, select all the text, and paste it into the box above.
Hit "Parse workouts" — all your workouts will appear in a list. Check the ones you want and hit "Import selected."
